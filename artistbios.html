Artist Bios





Dodie

Dodie Clark is a 25 year old singer and songwriter
who has released three EPs, beginning with Intertwined in 2016,
then You in 2017, and finally Human in 2019
She has also recently released two singles, Guiltless
and Boys Like You. Dodie continues to be active on her YouTube channel
doddleoddle where she started her career.

Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift, 30, is a well-known country and pop singer and songwriter.
She released her first album, Taylor Swift
in 2006, Fearless in 2008, and Speak Now in 2010.
Those albums were country, and her next album, Red, in 2012 began
to have some pop elements. In 2014 she released 1989 which she said was officially a
pop album. Her next albums were Reputation in 2017 and finally Lover in 2019.

fun.

Fun is a band made up of Nate Ruess, Andrew Dost, and Jack Antonoff.
The band was formed in 2008 after Nate Ruess' former band split up. Fun has
released two albums, Aim and Ignite in 2009 and Some Nights
in 2012. They are mainly known for the songs We Are Young and
Some Nights, both from the second album.
