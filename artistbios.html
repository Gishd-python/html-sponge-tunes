Artist Bios


These are short bios of some of the artists whose music is featured on this website.
Not all of the artists on this site are written about here.

Dodie

Dodie Clark is a 25 year old singer and songwriter who has released three EPs, beginning with Intertwined in 2016, then You in 2017, and finally Human in 2019 She has also recently released two singles, Guiltlessand Boys Like You. Dodie continues to be active on her YouTube channel doddleoddle where she started her career.

Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift, 30, is a well-known country and pop singer and songwriter. She released her first album, Taylor Swift in 2006, Fearless in 2008, and Speak Now in 2010. Those albums were country, and her next album, Red, in 2012 began to have some pop elements. In 2014 she released 1989 which she said was officially a pop album. Her next albums were Reputation in 2017 and finally Lover in 2019.

fun.

Fun is a band made up of Nate Ruess, Andrew Dost, and Jack Antonoff. The band was formed in 2008 after Nate Ruess' former band split up. Fun has released two albums, Aim and Ignite in 2009 and Some Nights in 2012. They are mainly known for the songs We Are Young and Some Nights, both from the second album.

Cavetown

Cavetown, whose real name is Robin Skinner, is a singer and songwriter. Similar to Dodie, Cavetown began his career by releasing songs on YouTube. His first single was This is Home in 2015, and his first album was cavetown also in 2015. He then released 16/04/16 in 2016 and Lemon Boy in 2018. His 2019 mixtape Animal Kingdom was 10 songs from 5 collaborations. In 2020 he released Sleepyhead, his first album under a record label.

Clairo

Claire Cottrill, known as Clairo, is a bedroom pop singer and singwriter. She is probably best known for her lo-fi song Pretty Girl. Clairo released her first EP, which included Pretty Girl in 2018, titled diary 001 and then Immunity in 2019. She also wrote and performed the song Heaven for Skate Kitchen.
